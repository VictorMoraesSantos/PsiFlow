# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PsiFlow is used by psychologists, clinical staff, and small mental-health practices while managing day-to-day care: patient records, agenda, sessions, online appointments, notifications, and clinical documentation. Users need a calm workspace that supports attention, confidentiality, and fast recovery of context between consultations.

## Product Purpose

PsiFlow centralizes clinical operations for psychology care. Success means a professional can understand the day, open a patient context, prepare or record a session, and follow pending operational tasks without switching between disconnected tools.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, trustworthy. The interface should feel composed and human, with enough clinical seriousness for sensitive information but without becoming cold or bureaucratic.

## Anti-references

Avoid cold hospital software: generic blue-white surfaces, dense legacy tables, and sterile visual language. Avoid generic SaaS dashboard tropes: interchangeable metric cards, decorative gradients, and visual noise that competes with clinical work.

## Design Principles

1. Keep clinical context close: patient, appointment, and session state should be visible where decisions are made.
2. Prioritize calm focus: use restraint, readable hierarchy, and clear task boundaries.
3. Make status explicit: appointment, session, notification, and record states should be understandable without guessing.
4. Support interruption recovery: screens should help users resume work after a consultation, call, or administrative task.
5. Respect sensitive data: avoid playful treatment of private clinical information and make destructive actions clear.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain strong contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic controls, reduced-motion alternatives, and status communication that does not rely on color alone.
